Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines.
It enables systems to learn, reason, and make decisions. 
LangChain is a framework that helps developers build AI applications using large language models (LLMs).
LlamaIndex provides tools to index, retrieve, and query data efficiently for context-aware question answering.
Together, they can be used to build powerful multimodal AI chatbots that understand both text and images.
